Continued Dashboard Development

Better alerts, more guidance, and the best user experience possible to bridge the finance gap between crypto an fiat.

Gain Access to your Locked Collateral

Collateralized Debt Contracts or Vaults allow users to borrow crypto. This borrowing can only be used within the Maker framework, which prevents many usecases for borrowing. We believe that with a Proxy contract for the liquidiation event, we can satisfy the collateral requirements and lend out asset backed crypto that could be spent (or converted to Fiat).

Uncollateralized Borrowing

The first step in uncollateralized borrowing is knowing your Net Worth position. We believe that staking ones net worth amount provided by NewFi will allow individuals to borrow without collateral being held away from the user.
